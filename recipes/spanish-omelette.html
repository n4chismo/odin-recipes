Spanish Omelette

Description:

Transport your senses to the sunny plazas of Spain with each decadent bite of this iconic Spanish Omelette. A simple-yet-sublime combination of velvety eggs enveloping a filling of tender potatoes and crisp onions sautéed in a fragrant blend of olive oil and smoked paprika. This rustic delight is then lovingly cradled by a fluffy layer of eggs creating an irresistible handheld meal. Perfectly portable, this protein-packed Spanish Omelette is an effortless crowd-pleaser that can be whipped up in a flash using just a few kitchen staples. One taste transports you to a Spanish tapas bar without ever leaving home.

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 lb Yukon gold potatoes, peeled and diced into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (optional garnish)

Steps:

  1. Place the diced potatoes in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce heat to medium and simmer for 5 minutes until potatoes are barely tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a 9-inch non-stick skillet, heat 2 tbsp olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onions and sauté for 5 minutes until translucent.
  3. Add the par-boiled potato cubes and smoked paprika. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are lightly browned. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. In a medium bowl, beat the 6 eggs until blended. Pour over the potato-onion mixture and let sit for 1 minute to set the bottom.
  5. As the edges set, use a spatula to gently pull them toward the center, tilting the pan to allow the uncooked egg to flow to the edges.
  6. When the omelette is mostly set but still a little runny on top, remove from heat. Cover with a plate and carefully invert to release the omelette onto the plate.
  7. Slide the omelette back into the skillet using remaining 2 tbsp olive oil. Cook for 2 more minutes.
  8. Slide the Spanish Tortilla onto a plate and garnish with chopped parsley if desired. Allow to cool slightly before slicing into wedges.

Serve the robust, potato-packed Spanish Omelette warm or at room temp with bread, salad or your favorite tapas! It's an easy, portable meal.
