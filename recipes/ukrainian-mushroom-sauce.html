Ukrainian Mushroom Sauce

Description:

Indulge in the rich, velvety decadence of Ukrainian Mushroom Sauce - a traditional Eastern European delight that elevates any dish to new heights of flavor. This versatile sauce is a masterful blend of earthy mushrooms sautéed to perfection, melding seamlessly with aromatic onions, garlic, and a hint of tangy sour cream. The result is a creamy, umami-packed elixir that will have your taste buds doing a happy dance. Best of all, this mouthwatering sauce is incredibly easy to whip up, making it an effortless way to add a burst of comforting, savory goodness to your favorite proteins, pastas, or vegetables. One taste, and you'll be transported to the heartwarming kitchens of Ukraine.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour/li>
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth/li>
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a skillet, melt the butter over medium-high heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté for 5 minutes until they start to brown.
  2. Add the diced onions and cook for 2 more minutes until translucent. Then add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Sprinkle the flour over the mushroom mixture and stir to coat everything evenly. Cook for 2 minutes to cook out the raw flour taste.
  4. Gradually whisk in the broth and bring to a simmer. Let simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring frequently, until the sauce thickens to your desired consistency.
  5. Remove sauce from heat and stir in the sour cream until fully incorporated and smooth.
  6. Stir in the chopped fresh dill and season to taste with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve the mushroom sauce warm over meat, potatoes, pasta, or vegetables. Enjoy!

The roux helps create a velvety, thick mushroom gravy that is so satisfying.
